Our Mission

To enrich the lives of Minnesotans by making local history accessible, engaging, and educational through immersive experiences and digital storytelling.

Our Vision

To inspire and empower every Minnesotan by utilizing technology to create an inclusive, immersive, and enlightening journey through local history.

We aspire to bring Minnesota's local history to life, making it accessible and meaningful for everyone through hands-on experiences and digital storytelling. From bustling cities to small rural towns, we share the countless stories that make our state unique.

Minnesota's story isn't just about the prominent figures in history books. It's about the immigrant families who built our communities, the workers who shaped our industries, and the everyday heroes who stood up for what they believed in.

We believe in giving voice to all parts of our state's history—celebrating the diversity of experiences that have shaped Minnesota.
