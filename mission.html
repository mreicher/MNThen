Explore Our Mission

Purpose

To offer a universally accessible platform that brings Minnesota's rich history to life, connecting people across the state to the stories that shaped their communities and beyond.

Mission

To inspire and connect communities throughout Minnesota by bringing local history to life through accessible, engaging, and educational digital experiences.

Vision

To provide an inclusive, immersive, and enlightening educational experience, leveraging technology to help uncover and explore Minnesota's rich history.

Our Values

Wonder

We celebrate the power of curiosity, encouraging a sense of awe and discovery in every corner of Minnesota's history.

Inquiry

We value thoughtful questioning and research, fostering a deeper understanding of the past through exploration and critical thinking.

Engagement

We prioritize meaningful connections, inviting individuals and communities to actively participate in the shared journey of uncovering Minnesota's stories.

We bring Minnesota's local history to life through engaging experiences and digital storytelling, making our shared past accessible and meaningful for everyone. From bustling cities to small rural towns, we share the countless stories that make our state extraordinary.

Minnesota's history is more than prominent figures found in textbooks. It's about the immigrant families who built our communities, the workers who shaped our industries, and the everyday heroes who stood up for change.

By preserving and sharing these diverse experiences, we honor all the voices that have shaped Minnesota's past and continue to influence its future. Together, we hope to create deeper connections to our history and to each other.
