Supporting Minnesota Then

Minnesota Then is a free online platform dedicated to preserving and sharing valuable historical information through self-guided tours. Our mission is to provide an immersive and engaging experience that celebrates Minnesota's remarkable history. However, to continue offering these valuable resources, we rely on the generous support of individuals like you.

If you have enjoyed the content on Minnesota Then and find it valuable, we gratefully accept donations through Buy Me a Coffee. Your contributions go directly towards supporting the maintenance and development of our platform, ensuring that future generations can explore and appreciate Minnesota's rich cultural tapestry.

Your support plays a vital role in our mission to preserve and share Minnesota's history. Together, we can continue to celebrate and honor the legacy of our state. Thank you for being a part of the Minnesota Then community.
