The Chronarchivum Project

Multi-Faceted Research Study of a Single Historical Topic

About the Project

The Chronarchivum Project uses a multi-faceted research approach to document the history of a single topic. We attempt to leave no stone unturned, learning everything we can using a wide range of primary, secondary, and online sources.

The Research Topic

This history of the Hamm’s Brewery is well over a century long. It began no long after St. Paul became a city, and soon became a prominent part of its growth. It is the story of a family, a wildly successful business, and the community to grew around it.
