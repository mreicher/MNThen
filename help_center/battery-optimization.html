Battery Optimization Guide

Museum Without Walls

Important: Museum Without Walls is designed to be experienced on a mobile device. For the best experience, please use the web app on your smartphone or tablet.

This guide helps maximize battery life while using Museum Without Walls.

Note: Location services and tracking functionality are essential for Museum Without Walls to work properly. This guide focuses only on battery optimization techniques that won't interfere with the app's core features.

Why Battery Optimization Matters

Museum Without Walls uses several battery-intensive features to provide an immersive experience:

  • GPS and Location Services: Tracking your location to provide relevant content
  • Screen Display: Keeping your screen on to show exhibits and information
  • Mobile Browser: Running the web app in your browser
  • Data Connection: Maintaining an internet connection to load content

By optimizing your device's battery usage in other areas, you can enjoy the full Museum Without Walls experience without worrying about your device running out of power during your visit.

Battery-Saving Tips While Maintaining Full Functionality

⚡ Before Your Visit

  • Fully charge your device before starting your museum experience
  • Close other browser tabs to reduce memory and battery usage
  • Close background apps that might drain battery
  • Consider bringing a portable charger for extended visits
  • Adjust screen brightness to a lower but comfortable level
  • Turn off unnecessary connectivity like Bluetooth if you're not using it

⚡ During Your Visit

  • Use headphones: Playing audio through headphones uses less power than device speakers
  • Avoid switching between apps: Constantly switching between apps uses more battery
  • Reduce screen brightness: Lower your screen brightness in indoor environments
  • Use Wi-Fi when available: Wi-Fi typically uses less battery than cellular data
  • Keep your phone cool: Avoid leaving your device in direct sunlight as heat increases battery drain
Browser-Specific Battery Optimization
Mobile Chrome
1 Reduce Tab Usage

Manage your tabs to save battery:

  1. Tap the tab switcher icon (square) in the top-right
  2. Close any unnecessary tabs
  3. Keep only the Museum Without Walls tab open
2 Close Unused Apps

Free up system resources:

  1. Swipe up from the bottom of your screen and hold (or tap the square navigation button)
  2. Swipe away apps you're not using
  3. This frees up memory and reduces background processing
Safari (iOS)
1 Manage Tabs

Close unnecessary tabs:

  1. Tap the tab switcher icon in the bottom-right
  2. Swipe left on tabs you don't need to close them
  3. Or tap and hold the tab button and select Close All Tabs
2 Close Background Apps

Free up system resources:

  1. Double-click the home button (or swipe up and hold on devices without a home button)
  2. Swipe up on app previews to close apps you're not using
  3. This can help reduce battery drain from background processes
Firefox Mobile
1 Manage Tabs

Close unnecessary tabs:

  1. Tap the tab counter to view all open tabs
  2. Tap the "X" on tabs you want to close
  3. Or tap the menu and select Close All Tabs
2 Close Other Apps

Free up system resources:

  1. Exit Firefox and close other apps running in the background
  2. This ensures maximum resources are available for Museum Without Walls
Device-Specific Battery Optimization
Android Devices
1 Use Battery Saver Mode Carefully

Android's Battery Saver can help, but use it with caution:

  1. Swipe down from the top of your screen to access Quick Settings
  2. Tap the Battery Saver icon
  3. Or go to Settings > Battery > Battery Saver

Important: Some versions of Battery Saver may restrict location services. If Museum Without Walls stops working properly after enabling Battery Saver, you may need to disable it.

2 Ensure Browser Has Location Permissions

Make sure your browser has the necessary permissions:

  1. Go to Settings > Apps > [Your Browser]
  2. Tap Permissions > Location
  3. Select Allow only while using the app or Allow all the time
3 Optimize Browser Battery Usage

Ensure your browser isn't restricted too much:

  1. Go to Settings > Apps > [Your Browser]
  2. Tap Battery
  3. Select Optimize or Unrestricted (not "Restricted")

Note for Samsung devices: You can use the Device Care feature (Settings > Device Care > Battery) to optimize battery usage for apps other than your browser, which can help extend overall battery life.

iOS Devices (iPhone & iPad)
1 Use Low Power Mode Carefully

iOS Low Power Mode can help, but be aware of its limitations:

  1. Go to Settings > Battery
  2. Toggle on Low Power Mode
  3. Alternatively, ask Siri to "Turn on Low Power Mode"

Important: Low Power Mode may affect location services accuracy. If you notice issues with Museum Without Walls, you may need to disable Low Power Mode.

2 Ensure Precise Location is Enabled

For Museum Without Walls to work properly:

  1. Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services
  2. Find and tap your browser (Safari, Chrome, etc.)
  3. Ensure Precise Location is toggled ON
3 Check Battery Usage

See which apps are using battery and close unnecessary ones:

  1. Go to Settings > Battery
  2. Review the list of apps using battery
  3. Close any high-consumption apps you don't need
Troubleshooting Battery Drain Issues

If you're experiencing excessive battery drain while using Museum Without Walls:

Common Solutions

  1. Restart your device: This can resolve temporary system issues affecting battery performance
  2. Check for overheating: If your device is hot, take a break and allow it to cool down
  3. Check signal strength: Poor cellular signal causes devices to use more power trying to maintain connection
  4. Use Wi-Fi instead of cellular data: When available, Wi-Fi typically uses less battery
  5. Clear browser cache:
    • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Clear Browsing Data
    • Safari: Settings > Safari > Clear History and Website Data
    • Firefox: Settings > Delete browsing data
  6. Check for system updates: Ensure your device's operating system is up to date
  7. Reduce screen brightness: Lower your screen brightness to the minimum comfortable level
Additional Battery-Saving Tips

General Device Tips

  • Enable Dark Mode: If your browser and device support it, dark mode can save battery on OLED screens
  • Disable Vibration: Turn off vibration feedback to save battery
  • Use Airplane Mode with Wi-Fi: In areas with poor cellular reception but good Wi-Fi, enable Airplane Mode and then turn Wi-Fi back on
  • Adjust Auto-Lock/Screen Timeout: Set your screen to turn off after a shorter period of inactivity when not actively using the app
  • Disable Unnecessary Connectivity: Turn off Bluetooth, NFC, and other connectivity features you're not using
  • Keep Your Device Cool: Heat accelerates battery drain; keep your device out of direct sunlight
  • Carry a Portable Charger: For extended museum visits, bring a portable battery pack

Remember: Location services, JavaScript, and tracking functionality must remain enabled for Museum Without Walls to function properly. The tips in this guide focus on optimizing other aspects of your device's battery usage while maintaining essential functionality.
