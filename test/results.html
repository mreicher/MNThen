Help Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Minnesota Then, your digital Museum Without Walls. Click "Enter" to access the interactive map with historical markers.

Using the Map

Our interactive map displays historical markers across Minnesota. Each marker is color-coded by category. Click "Color Key" to view categories.

Exploring Sites

Click any marker to view detailed information about that historical site, including photos, descriptions, and historical context.
