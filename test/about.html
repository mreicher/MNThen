Our Story

Uncovering the stories of Minnesota's past — and making them accessible to everyone, everywhere.

A museum without walls.

Minnesota Then is a free, location-based digital history museum. Using your phone or browser, you can discover the stories behind the places around you — from historic landmarks to forgotten events — right where they happened. No tickets, no walls, no cost. Just history, accessible to everyone.

Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To inspire and connect Minnesota communities by bringing local history to life through engaging, accessible digital experiences.

Vision Statement

To create inclusive, immersive educational experiences that harness technology to reveal and celebrate Minnesota's rich, diverse history.

History should be accessible to everyone.

Understanding local history strengthens our future. By making Minnesota's past accessible, we help people connect with their roots, learn from yesterday, and shape a better tomorrow.

Our journey was inspired by a deep desire to tell the stories of places that can't always share their own past. We believe history should be open to everyone, not just those able to visit traditional museums. From early experiments to this digital platform, we've embraced innovative technology and thoughtful design to make Minnesota's rich history come alive — right at your fingertips.

Our Guiding Principles

Accessibility

Ensure Minnesota's history is open to all, wherever they are and whatever their resources. No tickets, no hours, no walls between you and the past.

Community

Strengthen connections through shared stories and collective memory. Built by Minnesotans, for Minnesotans — every story matters.

Innovation

Leverage technology to create immersive, engaging encounters with the past that go beyond what traditional museums can offer.

Education

Promote understanding and curiosity through thoughtful historical content that brings context and meaning to the world around us.

Creating Lasting Impact

Sharing our history — from headline-making moments to the everyday stories that shaped our communities — can create real change. With each story shared, each voice heard, we deepen our understanding of the path that brought us here.

Through these diverse perspectives and experiences, we gain insights that help us build a stronger future together. It's more than just preserving the past; it's about bringing people together, sparking meaningful conversations, and strengthening the connections between neighbors through our shared Minnesota story.

Our Approach

1

Research & Documentation

Meticulously researching and documenting Minnesota's history from diverse sources and perspectives.

2

Digital Preservation

Transforming historical content into engaging, accessible digital formats for everyone, everywhere.

3

Place-Based Experiences

Connecting stories to real places, so history comes alive where it actually happened.

4

Community Engagement

Involving communities in sharing and preserving their own stories to create a more inclusive, authentic historical record.

From thesis project to museum without walls.

2016

Founded Minnesota Then

Started as a post-grad thesis project to share local history on a community level.

2020

Launched the Blog

Began publishing in-depth articles about Minnesota's past — true crime, grand homes, forgotten landmarks, and the people who shaped the state.

2022

Expanded to Multimedia

The Museum Without Walls project enters active development, combining GPS, audio, and research into a single experience.

2025

Released the Web App

The Museum Without Walls v1 beta goes live — opening Minnesota history to everyone, everywhere, for free.
