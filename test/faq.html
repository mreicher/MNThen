Our Mission

Making Minnesota's history accessible, engaging, and meaningful for everyone.

Our Foundation

Mission Statement

To inspire and connect communities throughout Minnesota by bringing local history to life through accessible, engaging, and educational digital experiences.

Vision Statement

To provide an inclusive, immersive, and enlightening educational experience, leveraging technology to help uncover and explore Minnesota's rich history.

Why It Matters

Understanding our history helps us build a stronger future. By making Minnesota's history accessible, we empower individuals and communities to connect with their roots, learn from the past, and shape a better tomorrow. Every story preserved is a bridge between generations, every voice heard strengthens our collective understanding of who we are and where we're going.

Our Guiding Principles

Accessibility

Making history available to everyone, regardless of location, resources, or background. We believe knowledge should have no barriers.

Community

Building connections through shared stories and experiences. Together, we create a richer understanding of our collective past.

Innovation

Using cutting-edge technology to create engaging historical experiences that bring the past to life in meaningful ways.

Education

Fostering deep understanding through meaningful historical content that inspires curiosity and lifelong learning.

Creating Lasting Impact

Sharing our history—from headline-making moments to the everyday stories that shaped our communities—creates real change. With each story shared and each voice heard, we deepen our understanding of the path that brought us here.

Through these diverse perspectives and experiences, we gain insights that help us build a stronger future together. It's more than just preserving the past; it's about bringing people together, sparking meaningful conversations, and strengthening the connections between neighbors through our shared Minnesota story.

Our Approach

1

Research & Documentation

We meticulously research and document historical events, places, and stories from diverse sources and perspectives across Minnesota, ensuring accuracy and inclusivity in every narrative.

2

Digital Preservation

We transform historical content into engaging digital formats that can be accessed by anyone, anywhere, ensuring preservation for future generations while making history interactive and immersive.

3

Location-Based Experiences

We create immersive experiences that connect digital content to physical locations, allowing visitors to experience history where it happened and feel the authentic connection to place.

4

Community Engagement

We actively involve communities in sharing and preserving their own histories, creating a more inclusive and authentic historical record that reflects the true diversity of Minnesota's story.
