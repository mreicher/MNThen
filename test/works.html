How It Works

Your step-by-step guide to exploring Minnesota's history wherever you are.

Reimagining the Museum

The Museum Without Walls is an innovative, location-based experience that brings Minnesota's history to life—right where it happened. Unlike traditional museums confined to buildings, our exhibits exist outdoors at the actual sites of historical events. Using your smartphone and our interactive map, you can explore and discover Minnesota's rich history wherever you are.

The Process

History unlocks when you arrive.

Your smartphone's GPS guides you to these historical locations and unlocks immersive audio content when you arrive. Here's how to use it:

Step 01

Open Web App

Visit our website and click the link to open the Museum Without Walls web app on your smartphone. No app store required.

Step 02

Navigate the Map

You'll see an interactive map with your location marked. Pinch, pan, and zoom around to discover historical locations near you.

Step 03

Find Interesting Sites

When you find a location that interests you, tap on it to see a summary. Use the directions button to open your preferred navigation app.

Step 04

Experience History

Once you're within 20 feet of the site, a location page will automatically open. Press play to listen to the audio tour guide.

Step 05

Explore More Sites

After the audio finishes, return to the map and discover more historical locations. Each site offers a unique story about Minnesota's rich history.

Features

Built for the modern explorer.

Location-Based Experience

Our exhibits exist at the exact locations where history happened, creating a more authentic and immersive experience.

AI-Powered Audio Guides

AI-generated audio narration brings history to life through engaging storytelling when you arrive at each location.

Always Available

Visit historical sites on your own schedule - our digital museum is open 24/7, every day of the year.

Pro Tips

Before you head out.

  • Charge your device — Make sure your smartphone has enough battery for your exploration.
  • Use headphones — For the best audio experience, especially in noisy environments or windy outdoors.
  • Enable location services — The app needs your precise location to unlock the content when you arrive.
  • Plan your visits — Check the map beforehand to find clusters of historical sites you can visit in one trip.
  • Share your experience — Take photos and share your Museum Without Walls journey on social media using #MinnesotaThen.

Ready to explore?

The full map is free. No app store required — open it in your phone's browser, travel to the site, and step into history.
