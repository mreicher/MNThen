Minnesota Historical Timeline

Key events that shaped our state's history

1858

Minnesota Becomes a State

Minnesota was admitted as the 32nd state of the Union on May 11, 1858, created from the eastern half of the Minnesota Territory.

1862

U.S.-Dakota War

A brief but violent conflict between the United States and the Dakota Sioux, resulting in significant changes to Minnesota's Native American relations.

1889

Pillsbury A Mill Opens

The completion of the Pillsbury A Mill in Minneapolis, which would become the world's largest flour mill and cement Minneapolis's status as the "Flour Milling Capital of the World."

1965

Viking Stadium Opens

The opening of Metropolitan Stadium in Bloomington, home to the Minnesota Vikings and Twins, marking a new era in Minnesota sports.
