Kidnapping of Edward Bremer (Jan 17 - Feb 7, 1934)

By: Matt Reicher

Commercial State Bank President Edward Bremer faced a chilling kidnapping by the Barker-Karpis Gang in January 1934. After a tense three weeks and a $200,000 ransom, Bremer was released. His cooperation, details, and the national focus led to the gang's downfall.
