The Plantation Night Club (1930 - 1933)

Creator: Matt Reicher

The Plantation Night Club in White Bear Lake, Minnesota, was a popular Prohibition-era venue attracting locals and gangsters. Despite its notoriety, the club struggled after Prohibition ended and eventually closed in 1936. The building was demolished in 1974, and Lion's Park now stands in its place..
