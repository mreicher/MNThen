Grain Belt Beer Sign

Creator: Matt Reicher

The iconic Grain Belt Beer sign, originally placed on the Marigold Ballroom in Minneapolis in 1941, underwent a series of relighting efforts after years of darkness. Schell's Brewing led restoration and celebrated its relighting in 2017 with LED lights and neon tubes.
