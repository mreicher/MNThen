A Spatial Exhibit Platform

Where Place Becomes Presence

Stories, rooted in the ground where they unfolded

PathWeaver is a mobile web app that transforms physical spaces into immersive exhibits—no app store, no login, no backend. Just stories, anchored exactly where they belong.

The Challenge of Place-Based Storytelling

Museums, historic sites, and cultural organizations face a fundamental tension: how do you make a place speak without losing what makes it sacred?

The Problem

Traditional wayfinding treats locations as waypoints. Apps demand downloads, permissions, and compromise visitor privacy. Enterprise GIS platforms prioritize data visualization over emotional resonance. Meanwhile, physical plaques and brochures can't adapt, update, or layer contested narratives.

The PathWeaver Approach

A presence-aware platform that activates stories when visitors arrive—not before. Built entirely in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, it runs in any browser with no installation barrier. Content updates require only basic file editing. The technology recedes, letting place and narrative take center stage.

Built for Storytelling, Not Just Mapping

Every feature serves a single purpose: to help visitors encounter place with depth and dignity.

Presence-Aware Activation

Content reveals itself when visitors are physically at a location—creating moments of discovery rather than distraction.

Zero-Install Web App

Runs in any modern browser. No app store friction, no permission requests, no barriers between curiosity and engagement.

Jitter-Free Performance

Optimized GPS tracking with smooth icon movement tested at highway speeds. Works reliably on mid-tier devices.

Privacy by Design

No tracking, no analytics, no data collection. Visitors explore on their own terms with complete anonymity.

White-Label Ready

Fully customizable visual identity. Your colors, typography, and voice—the platform adapts to your brand.

Community-Centered

Designed to support inclusive narratives, contested histories, and living stories that evolve with community input.

Technical Foundation

Battle-tested architecture that prioritizes reliability, accessibility, and maintainability.

Proven in the Field

PathWeaver powers Minnesota Then, a statewide historical platform with 150+ locations across diverse geography and demographics.

Real-World Performance

Tested extensively across urban cores, rural highways, and everything between. Handles variable network conditions, different device capabilities, and the unpredictability of actual human navigation.

Accessibility-First Design

Screen reader compatible, keyboard navigable, and designed for cognitive accessibility. Works on older devices still common in under-resourced communities.

Low-Maintenance Operations

Content updates require basic HTML skills or a simple static site generator. No database management, no API versioning, no security patches—just files on a server.

Sustainable Architecture

Built on open web standards that have proven stable for decades. No vendor lock-in, no recurring platform fees, no risk of a third-party service shutting down.

Why Not Use Existing Solutions?

We evaluated every major platform. None aligned with the values place-based storytelling deserves.

Native Apps (iOS/Android)

  • App store approval delays content updates
  • Installation barrier loses 80% of potential visitors
  • Requires ongoing maintenance for OS updates
  • Demands intrusive device permissions

Google My Maps / ArcGIS StoryMaps

  • Data visualization prioritized over narrative
  • Limited control over user experience flow
  • Platform branding and analytics embedded
  • Not optimized for real-time location awareness

Audio Tour Platforms

  • Assumes linear progression through space
  • Audio-only excludes accessibility needs
  • Expensive per-user licensing models
  • Difficult to update or iterate content

QR Code Systems

  • Requires physical infrastructure installation
  • Weather and vandalism create maintenance burden
  • No ability to activate based on proximity
  • Breaks in low-visibility or winter conditions

PathWeaver Delivers What Others Can't

Instant access without installation. Privacy without surveillance. Narrative depth without data-first constraints. Community control without vendor dependence. Place-based activation that actually works in the field.
