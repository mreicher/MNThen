Museum Without Walls

Our Museum Without Walls concept brings history out of traditional museum spaces and into the real world. Through augmented reality and interactive displays, we transform everyday locations into living history exhibits.

Experience historical events, meet virtual historical figures, and see how Minnesota's landscape has changed over time - all from your smartphone or tablet.
