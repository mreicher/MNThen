About Minnesota Then

Welcome to Minnesota Then: Community Markers! This interactive map allows you to explore and contribute to the rich tapestry of personal and community history across Minnesota.

Click on markers to learn about different locations and their significance to our community. If you're logged in, you can add new markers by clicking on the map.

Welcome to Community Markers

This is a place for people to mark their history, their family's history, and the history of their friends and community across Minnesota. Explore existing markers or log in to add your own!

How to Freely Host Your Images

To add images to your markers, you'll need to host them online. Here are some free image hosting options:

  1. Imgur: A popular image hosting site.
    • Go to imgur.com
    • Click "New post" and upload your image
    • Right-click on the uploaded image and select "Copy image address"
  2. ImgBB: Another free image hosting service.
    • Visit imgbb.com
    • Click "Start uploading" and select your image
    • Copy the "Direct link" provided
  3. Postimages: A simple, free image hosting site.
    • Go to postimages.org
    • Choose your image and upload it
    • Copy the "Direct link" for your image

After hosting your image, paste the image URL into the "Image URL" field when adding a new marker.
