About Minnesota Then

Welcome to Minnesota Then: Community Markers! This interactive map allows you to explore and contribute to the rich tapestry of personal and community history across Minnesota.

Click on markers to learn about different locations and their significance to our community. If you're logged in, you can add new markers by clicking on the map.

Help us build a comprehensive map of Minnesota's personal and shared histories!
