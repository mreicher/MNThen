How It Works

  1. Click on the map to add a new history pin at that location.
  2. Fill in the details about your historical event or memory.
  3. Upload an image related to your pin.
  4. View other users' pins by clicking on the markers.
  5. Like and interact with pins to share your interest.
  6. Use the search function to find specific pins.
