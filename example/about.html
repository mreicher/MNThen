Welcome to Minnesota Then

We offer a variety of immersive and engaging experiences that help bring Minnesota's past to life. Explore at your own pace with self-guided tours, travel the state visiting our Museum Without Walls, test your knowledge with trivia games, and more.

"Minnesota Then brings the past to life through my phone, making learning about local history an exciting adventure."

— Bill Larrabee, St. Paulite

Our Mission

To enrich the lives of Minnesotans by making local history accessible, engaging, and educational through immersive experiences and digital storytelling.

Our Vision

To inspire and empower every Minnesotan by utilizing technology to create an inclusive, immersive, and enlightening journey through local history.

We aspire to bring Minnesota's local history to life, making it accessible and meaningful for everyone through hands-on experiences and digital storytelling. From bustling cities to small rural towns, we share the countless stories that make our state unique. Whether you're a lifelong resident or a newcomer, we create opportunities for you to connect with Minnesota's past in ways that resonate today.

Minnesota's story isn't just about the prominent figures in history books. It's about the immigrant families who built our communities, the workers who shaped our industries, and the everyday heroes who stood up for what they believed in. These lesser-known stories are just as crucial to understanding who we are as Minnesotans.

We believe in giving voice to all parts of our state's history—celebrating the diversity of experiences that have shaped Minnesota. By preserving and sharing stories from every corner of our state, from all communities and backgrounds, we strengthen our connections to each other and our shared history. Together, we're building a more complete picture of Minnesota's past that will (hopefully) inspire future generations.
