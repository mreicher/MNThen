Our Mission

Making Minnesota's history accessible, engaging, and meaningful for everyone.

Who We Are

We are dedicated historians, storytellers, and technology enthusiasts committed to preserving and sharing Minnesota's rich history in innovative ways.

What We Do

We create accessible digital experiences that bring Minnesota's history to life, connecting communities with their local heritage through engaging storytelling and technology.

Why We Do It

We believe that understanding our shared history creates stronger communities and helps shape a better future for all Minnesotans.

Our Guiding Principles

Accessibility

Making history available to everyone, regardless of location or resources.

Community

Building connections through shared stories and experiences.

Innovation

Using technology to create engaging historical experiences.

Education

Fostering understanding through meaningful historical content.

Creating Lasting Impact

Through our digital platform, we're breaking down barriers to historical knowledge and creating new ways for Minnesotans to connect with their heritage. Every story we share helps build a more informed, connected, and engaged community.
