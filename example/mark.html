How to Host Images

You can use the following services to host your images:

  • Imgur:
    1. Go to imgur.com
    2. Click "New post" and upload your image
    3. Right-click on the uploaded image and select "Copy image address"
    4. Paste the copied URL into the Image URL field
  • ImgBB:
    1. Visit imgbb.com
    2. Click "Start uploading" and select your image
    3. Copy the "Direct link" provided
    4. Paste the copied URL into the Image URL field

Remember to use direct image links ending with .jpg, .png, etc.
