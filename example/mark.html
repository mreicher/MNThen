        Image URL Information

        Free Image Hosting Services:

        • Imgur - Easy to use, no account required
        • PostImages - No registration needed, direct links available
        • ImgBB - Offers various image sizes and hotlinking
        • Flickr - Great for photographers, offers 1TB free storage

        To use an image, upload it to one of these services and copy the direct image URL. Paste this URL into the Image URL field when adding a marker.
