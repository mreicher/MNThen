About Minnesota Then

We bring history to life with immersive experiences in communities throughout the state.

About Us

Nestled amidst expansive lakes, dense forests, and cities big and small, Minnesota has witnessed a multitude of captivating stories unfold across its lands. These tales encompass the lives of remarkable individuals, the significance of key locations, and the many impactful events that shaped our state's past.

Minnesota Then is not just about revisiting history; it's about experiencing it. We believe that the past is not confined to textbooks or lecture halls; it's alive in the very soil we tread, the air we breathe, and the communities we live.

Our Mission

To make local history accessible to everyone, irrespective of age, background, or knowledge level. By transforming locations across the state into self-guided exhibit sites, we aim to provide a hands-on, immersive experience that both educates and inspires.

At Minnesota Then, our goal is to uncover these stories and share them with people willing to explore the very places where history came to life. We believe every step offers a story to tell about the state’s vibrant past - stories we strive to showcase to everyone intrigued by Minnesota history.

A Message from Matt

My name is Matt Reicher, I am the creator of Minnesota Then.

For my first message here, I'd like to share some background. Empowering accessibility within museum spaces has been a long-standing passion of mine. In 2016, my MA thesis project focused on leveraging alternative spaces to showcase local history exhibits, aiming to eliminate barriers of money, time, and distance. The primary goal was to make the rich stories of Minnesota’s past freely and inclusively available to all.

What you see today is only the beginning. As we grow and improve, our aim is not only to introduce compelling stories but continue to leverage technology to find new and interesting ways to make learning about Minnesota history worth the trip.

Thank you.
