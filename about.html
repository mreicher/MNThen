About Minnesota Then

Uncover the state's vibrant past, today

Minnesota Then celebrates the rich history of our state. Founded in 2012, we offer engaging experiences to explore Minnesota's past.

Discover self-guided tours through notable locations. Uncover the state's beer and brewing legacy with our Museum Without Walls. Test your knowledge with our trivia games.

Our blog provides insights into the people, places, and events that shaped Minnesota over the centuries. We also offer resources for digging deeper into the state's captivating history.

Whether a lifelong Minnesotan or visitor, Minnesota Then connects you with the stories that molded our great state.

Our Mission

To enrich the lives of Minnesotans by making local history accessible, engaging, and educational through immersive experiences and digital storytelling.

Our Vision

To inspire and empower every Minnesotan by utilizing technology to create an inclusive, immersive, and enlightening journey through local history.
