About Minnesota Then

Embark on an immersive journey into Minnesota's rich history with our self-guided tours of the state's most iconic neighborhoods and landmarks. Minnesota Then offers a unique opportunity to explore the diverse cultural heritage and fascinating past of the state.

With just a mobile device and a sense of adventure, you'll be transported through time and space to some of the most significant historical locations in Minnesota. From the bustling cityscapes of Minneapolis to the charming neighborhoods beyond the metro area, you'll witness firsthand the incredible evolution of Minnesota's cities.

But the adventure doesn't stop there. You'll also have the opportunity to immerse yourself in Minnesota's natural wonders, from picturesque lakes and rivers to rolling hills and verdant forests. Whether you're a history buff, a curious traveler, or a lifelong resident, our self-guided tours offer a fun and educational experience that will deepen your appreciation for Minnesota's rich heritage.

So why wait? Start your journey today and discover the hidden gems of Minnesota's past. With so much to see and explore, every moment of this unforgettable experience will be filled with awe and wonder!
