Minnesota Then

Embark on an Unforgettable Journey

Discover the rich history of Minnesota through our immersive self-guided tours of iconic neighborhoods and landmarks. Minnesota Then offers a unique opportunity to explore the fascinating past and diverse cultural heritage of the state.

With just a GPS-capable mobile device and a sense of adventure, you'll be transported through time and space to some of the most significant historical locations in Minnesota. From the vibrant cityscapes of Minneapolis to the charming neighborhoods beyond the metro area, you'll witness the incredible evolution of Minnesota's cities firsthand.

But the adventure doesn't stop there. Immerse yourself in Minnesota's natural wonders, from picturesque lakes and rivers to rolling hills and verdant forests. Our self-guided tours are designed for history buffs, curious travelers, and lifelong residents alike, providing a fun and educational experience that will deepen your appreciation for Minnesota's rich heritage.

So why wait? Start your journey today and uncover the hidden gems of Minnesota's past. With so much to see and explore, every moment of this unforgettable experience will be filled with awe and wonder!
