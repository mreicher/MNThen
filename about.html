About Us

Nestled amidst expansive lakes, dense forests, and cities big and small, Minnesota has witnessed a multitude of captivating stories unfold across its lands. These tales encompass the lives of remarkable individuals, the significance of key locations, and the many impactful events that shaped our state's past.

Minnesota Then is not just about revisiting history; it's about experiencing it. We believe that past is not confined to textbooks or lecture halls; it's alive in the very soil we tread, the air we breathe, and the communities we're part of.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make local history accessible to everyone, irrespective of age, background, or knowledge level. By transforming locations across the state into self-guided exhibit sites, we aim to provide a hands-on, immersive experience that both educates and inspires.
