Our Story

Making Minnesota's history accessible to everyone, everywhere.

At Minnesota Then, we’re passionate about making history accessible to everyone, regardless of their location, time constraints, or budget. By leveraging technology and creative storytelling, we break down the usual barriers that keep people from engaging with museums – like cost, distance, and scheduling. We’re excited to offer a unique way for everyone to connect with the past – anytime, wherever they are.

Why We Do This

Our journey to creating Minnesota Then was motivated by a long-standing belief that history should be available to everyone, not just those with the resources to visit traditional museums. Through innovative technology and thoughtful design, we've created a platform that brings Minnesota's rich history directly to your device.

Minnesota Then allows users to explore historic sites around the state, with detailed information, images, and audio tours accessible on their mobile devices. It's designed to be an easy, fun, and informative way to engage with the past, whether you're at home or out exploring.

An Invitation to Discover

We believe Minnesota Then is more than just an app; it's a vision for a new way of experiencing history—one without walls, limitations, or barriers. It's about making history personal, accessible, and meaningful to people of all ages and backgrounds.
