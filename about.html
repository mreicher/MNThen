Minnesota Then

Experience Minnesota History Like Never Before

Welcome to Minnesota Then. Nestled between vast lakes and sprawling forests, our state has borne witness to countless tales, from the footprints of past to the moments that help define the modern era. Here at Minnesota Then, we've embarked on a mission to bring these stories to light, ensuring the echoes of local history are not just heard, but also felt.

Minnesota Then is not just about revisiting history; it's about experiencing it. We believe that history is not confined to textbooks or lecture halls; it's alive in the very soil we tread, the air we breathe, and the communities we're part of. Our mission is to make local history accessible to everyone, irrespective of age, background, or knowledge level. By transforming significant locations across the state into self-guided exhibit sites, we aim to provide a hands-on, immersive experience that both educates and inspires.

At Minnesota Then, we recognize the diverse tapestry that constitutes our state's history. We strive to represent all voices, ensuring that each exhibit and tour is inclusive, comprehensive, and respectful. By collaborating with local historians, communities, and cultural organizations, we aim to present a holistic view of Minnesota's past.

Whether you're a history enthusiast, a curious traveler, or someone looking to rediscover their roots, Minnesota Then invites you to embark on this unparalleled journey. Let's traverse through time, uncover hidden tales, celebrate milestones, and ensure that the legacy of Minnesota's past shapes an informed and inspired future. Every step tells a story.
