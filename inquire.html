PathWeaver

Every Place Has a Story

We help you tell it exactly where it happened

After years of bringing Minnesota's stories to life, we've learned something special: history isn't just more engaging when you experience it where it unfolded—it's transformative. Now we're helping museums, cities, and cultural organizations create those same magical moments.

Built by Storytellers, for Storytellers

We handle the technology so you can focus on what matters—sharing stories that connect people to place.

Where History Lives

Your visitors don't just read about history—they walk through it. Our platform unlocks stories automatically as people explore, creating those "aha" moments that happen when you realize you're standing exactly where something meaningful occurred.

Stories That Stick

Combine photos, voices from the past, and interactive moments to create experiences people remember long after they leave. When stories connect to place, they become part of someone's personal journey—not just another fact they forget.

Community Building

Watch visitors become ambassadors as they share discoveries with friends and family. PathWeaver creates those "you've got to see this" moments that spread organically, bringing more people to experience your stories firsthand.

What We've Learned Along the Way

After bringing Minnesota's stories to life across dozens of locations, here's what surprises us every time:

"I thought I knew this city, but suddenly I'm seeing it through completely different eyes. Every corner has a story I never knew existed."

Maria Rodriguez
Visitor, St. Paul History Tour

"The difference between reading about history in a building versus standing where it happened is like the difference between hearing about the ocean and actually putting your feet in the sand."

Dr. James Thompson
History Professor, University of Minnesota

Here's the Thing About Place-Based Stories

They don't just inform people—they change how they see the world around them. When someone discovers that the coffee shop they visit every morning was once a speakeasy, or that their favorite park hosted civil rights meetings, something shifts. History stops being abstract and becomes personal.

That's what we help you create. Not just content, but connections. Not just information, but transformation.
