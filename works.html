Self-Guided Tours Tutorial

Learn how to navigate and explore Minnesota's history with ease.

How It Works

Step 1: Choose a Tour

Select a tour that interests you from our list of available tours. Navigate from the websiteâ€™s main page to the tour selection page and choose your preferred option.

Step 2: Agree to the Conditions

Read and agree to the conditions of the tour to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience. Safety is our top priority.

Step 3: Begin the Tour

Start your adventure by visiting the first location indicated on your tour map. Follow the numbered markers to explore each stop.

Step 4: Follow the Map

Use the provided map to navigate to each location on your tour. The map will guide you seamlessly from one stop to the next.

Step 5: Visit a Location

When you arrive at a tour stop, a new page will open with historical information, images, and audio. Learn about the location and return to the map to continue.

Step 6: Complete the Tour

Explore as many locations as you'd like. When you're ready, end your tour and share your experiences with others.
