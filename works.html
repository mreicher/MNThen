About

What Is The Museum Without Walls?

An innovative, location-based experience that brings Minnesota's history to life‚Äîright where it happened. Unlike traditional museums confined to buildings, our exhibits exist outdoors at the actual sites of historical events.

Getting Started

How It Works

Your smartphone's GPS guides you to historical locations and unlocks immersive audio content when you arrive, making history come alive through your own exploration.

1

Open the Web App

Visit our website and click the link to open the Museum Without Walls web app on your smartphone.

2

Navigate the Map

You'll see an interactive map with your location marked. Pinch, pan, and zoom around to discover historical locations.

3

Find Interesting Locations

When you find a location that interests you, tap on it to see a summary and use the directions button.

4

Experience the History

Once you're within 20 feet of the site, a location page will automatically open. Press play to listen to the audio tour.

5

Explore More Sites

After the audio finishes, return to the map and discover more historical locations. Each site offers a unique story.

History at Your Fingertips

Our intuitive interface makes discovering Minnesota's history effortless. Simply open the app, and you'll instantly see historical sites near you.

Each location is carefully researched and presented with engaging audio narration that brings the past to life.

Whether you're a lifelong Minnesotan or just visiting, you'll discover stories you never knew existed in places you pass every day.

Pro Tips

Get the Best Experience

Charge Your Device

Make sure your smartphone has enough battery for your exploration journey.

Use Headphones

For the best audio experience, especially in noisy outdoor environments.

Enable Location Services

The app needs your location to provide the full interactive experience.

Plan Your Visits

Check the map beforehand to find clusters of historical sites in one area.

Share Your Experience

Take photos and share your journey on social media using #MinnesotaThen.

Stay Safe

Be aware of your surroundings and follow all local traffic and safety rules.
