About Museum Without Walls

Museum Without Walls is our innovative digital initiative that breaks down traditional barriers to accessing Minnesota's history. Using cutting-edge technology, we bring historical sites and stories directly to you, making history accessible to everyone, anywhere, at any time.

Virtual Tours

Our virtual tours offer an immersive way to explore Minnesota's historic sites. Each tour combines rich storytelling, historical photographs, and interactive elements to create an engaging experience that brings the past to life. Whether you're a history enthusiast, student, or curious explorer, our tours provide a unique way to discover Minnesota's rich heritage from your device.
