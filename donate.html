Thank You for Visiting Minnesota Then!

We are deeply grateful for your interest in our mission to preserve and share Minnesota's rich history. Your support allows us to continue uncovering the stories that shape our state's past and make them accessible to everyone.

By considering a donation, you are helping us grow our resources, digitize historical documents, and create educational programs that inspire future generations. Together, we can ensure that Minnesota's history remains alive and accessible for years to come.
