How Your Support Helps

Your generosity creates a lasting impact on preserving Minnesota's history.

Historical Preservation

Your donation helps fund efforts to document, preserve, and protect Minnesota's historical sites, artifacts, and stories for future generations.

Educational Resources

Support the development of educational materials that make Minnesota's rich history accessible to students, researchers, and history enthusiasts.

Community Programs

Enable community-based initiatives that connect Minnesotans with their shared heritage through events, workshops, and collaborative projects.
