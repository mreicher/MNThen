Your donation helps make local history accessible to everyone. Consider supporting our work in uncovering and preserving the unique stories that shape our state's past.

Reasons to Give

  • Grow our resources: Your donation directly supports the expansion of our work, ensuring a richer historical experience for everyone.
  • Make history accessible: Help us break down barriers and provide free access to Minnesota's past for students, researchers, and curious minds.
  • Invest in research: Empower us to uncover hidden stories and bring new perspectives to light.
  • Preserve unique local treasures: Protect the artifacts, documents, and narratives that define Minnesota's diverse heritage.
