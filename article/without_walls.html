Beyond the Building: History Comes Alive with Our "Museum Without Walls"

By Matt Reicher

Have you ever felt like local history gets lost within the confines of a museum? Sure, artifacts and exhibits are fascinating, but wouldn't it be incredible to experience the past where it actually happened?

This is the revolutionary concept behind our "Museum Without Walls"! Imagine stepping back in time, not just reading about it in a dusty textbook. Picture yourself standing on the very ground where a pivotal moment unfolded, surrounded by the sights, sounds, and even textures of the era.

Minnesota's rich history comes alive with this innovative approach. Forget the traditional museum setting. Instead, the "Museum Without Walls" transforms the entire state into your exhibit hall. You become an active participant, no longer a passive observer.

Here's How It Works:

  1. Step 1: Explore the Map:
    • Head over to the Minnesota Then "Museum Without Walls" map page on your mobile device (https://mnthen.com/mnthen_welcome.html/).
    • Use the pinch and zoom function to explore the interactive map.
  2. Step 2: Discover Historical Locations:
    • As you zoom in and out, moving around the map, historical landmarks and sites will appear as markers. Each represents a location of significance in Minnesota's past. Tap the marker to see a pop-up containing basic information about the site.
  3. Step 3: Plan Your Visit:
    • To travel to the site to learn more, simply tap the "Directions" button.
    • This will activate your device's Google Maps app and provide you with turn-by-turn directions to get you right to the historical site.
  4. Step 4: Immerse Yourself in History:
    • When you are within a predetermined range, your phone will automatically launch the sites location page.
    • This page will transport you back in time through:
      • Historical Photos: Get a glimpse of what the site looked like in its prime through captivating photos from the past.
      • Virtual Tour Guide: Listen to an engaging audio story narrated by a virtual tour guide. They'll bring the location's history to life with vivid details and anecdotes.
  5. Step 5: Continue Your Journey:
    • Once you've finished exploring, simply exit and continue on the "Museum Without Walls" map. Discover new historical gems, embark on virtual journeys through time, and piece together Minnesota's rich past!

This museum isn't just about facts and figures. It's about sparking curiosity, igniting your imagination, and fostering a deeper connection to Minnesota's past. It allows you to truly understand how history shaped the communities you live in today. With it you can experience the past through the lens of the present.

So, ditch the textbooks and embrace the adventure! With our "Museum Without Walls," history isn't just something to be read about â€“ it's something to be experienced.

Let's break down the walls of history together!
