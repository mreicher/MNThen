Introducing Minnesota Then's Self-Guided Local History Tours

By Matt Reicher

Embark on a captivating journey through Minnesota's storied past with Minnesota Then's self-guided local history tours. Our immersive tours provide a seamless and engaging experience that celebrates our state's remarkable heritage. Delve into Minnesota's captivating narratives at your own pace, unlocking historical insights and exploring our rich cultural tapestry with just a GPS-capable mobile device.

Discover the unique stories and landmarks that shape our communities as Minnesota Then creates history tours statewide. From bustling neighborhoods to iconic landmarks, our tours span well-known and lesser-known historical sites, offering a comprehensive understanding of Minnesota's diverse history.

Accessible to everyone, our self-guided tours require only a GPS-capable mobile device. Choose a tour location, follow the map to markers, and let engaging audio narration bring historical sites to life. Immerse yourself in captivating stories, anecdotes, and insights, fostering a deeper appreciation for our state's remarkable heritage.

Take your time, explore hidden gems, and uncover untold tales with Minnesota Then's self-guided tours. Pause, reflect, and absorb the historical information that resonates with you as you navigate the tour route. Each step reveals new layers of Minnesota's history, creating a captivating experience that connects you with the vibrant past of our communities.
