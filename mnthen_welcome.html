Help Guide

Getting Started

Click "I Agree - Enter Museum" to accept the safety terms and access our interactive map featuring historical markers across Minnesota.

Using the Interactive Map

Our map displays historical markers throughout Minnesota. Each marker is color-coded by category. Tap any marker to preview information, then visit the location to unlock the full exhibit.

Exploring Historical Sites

When you arrive at a marked location, tap the marker to access detailed historical information including photos, audio narration, and interactive elements.

Desktop Users - Important Notice

This app is optimized for mobile phones and tablets. Location-based features may not function properly on desktop browsers. For the complete experience, please use a mobile device.
