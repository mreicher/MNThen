Help Guide

Getting Started

Welcome to Minnesota Then, your digital Museum Without Walls. This guide will help you navigate through our interactive historical experience.

Tip: Click "Start Exploring" to access the interactive map with all historical markers.

Using the Map

Our interactive map displays historical markers across Minnesota. Each marker is color-coded by category to help you find points of interest.

To view marker categories, click on "Marker Color Key" in the bottom navigation bar.

Exploring Historical Sites

Click on any marker to view detailed information about that historical site, including a photo, description, and historical context.

Safety Guidelines

Please remember to follow all safety guidelines when visiting historical sites:

  • Stay aware of your surroundings
  • Respect private property
  • Never use the app while driving
  • Follow all posted signs and regulations
