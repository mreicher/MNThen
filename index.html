A First-Hand Look

Explore Minnesota’s past through virtual exhibits and self-guided tours of the state’s iconic neighborhoods and landmarks. All you need to get started is a GPS-capable mobile device and a sense of adventure.

Who is it for?

  • History buffs
  • Tourists looking for an authentic experience
  • Local residents who want to learn more about their city
  • Students and teachers interested in state history
  • Anyone looking for a reason to get out of the house

How it Works

In this tutorial you will learn how to navigate the Minnesota Then web application to explore the rich history of Minnesota at your own pace. It provides step-by-step instructions to guide you through the touring process.

Take time to learn the information before you head out to see Minnesota’s rich history. A better understanding of the tour process will allow you to concentrate on enjoying the experience of uncovering the state’s hidden gems and untold tales along your route.
