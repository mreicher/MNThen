How It Works

History unlocks
when you arrive.

No app to download, no subscription. Open the map in your phone's browser, travel to a marked location, and the exhibit unlocks the moment you step on-site.

01

Open the Map

Open the web app on any smartphone. Every historic marker across Minnesota appears on the interactive map — ready for your field trip.

02

Travel to the Site

Navigate to any marked location. Your GPS is the key — arrive at the coordinates and the exhibit unlocks automatically.

03

Experience the History

Read the story, view historic photographs, and hear the audio docent. History is no longer somewhere else — it's right where you're standing.

How to close this app

PWA apps run without a browser bar. Pick your device below for the quickest way out.

Swipe up from the very bottom
Start at the bottom edge and flick upward in one motion. You'll go straight to the home screen.
or
Swipe up, then pause to switch apps
Swipe up and hold briefly at the middle of the screen. Find this app and swipe it upward to close it.
Swipe up from the bottom edge
A quick upward flick from the very bottom returns you to the home screen instantly.
or
Using three-button navigation?
Tap the circle (Home) button to leave, or the square (Recents) button then swipe this app away.
Click the close button
Press × in the window corner, or use Ctrl+W (Windows) / ⌘W (Mac).
or
Type a new address
Click the address bar at the top of the window and type any URL to navigate away.
