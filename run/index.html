About RunFlow

Welcome to RunFlow

RunFlow is your professional running companion, designed to help you track every step of your journey with precision and style. Whether you're a beginner taking your first steps or a seasoned marathoner, RunFlow provides the tools you need to achieve your goals.

Location Services

RunFlow uses your device's GPS to accurately track your running distance, pace, and route. Your location data is processed locally on your device and is never shared with third parties. You can disable location services at any time in your device settings.

Key Features

  • GPS Tracking
    High-accuracy GPS tracking for precise distance and pace measurements
  • Real-time Metrics
    Monitor your current pace, distance, and time as you run
  • Route Visualization
    View your running route on an interactive map after each session
  • Split Analysis
    Track your pace for each mile to improve your consistency
  • Achievements
    Earn badges and track your progress with milestone achievements
  • Social Sharing
    Share your running achievements on social media platforms

How to Use RunFlow

  • Start Your Run
    Press the Start button to begin GPS tracking and timer
  • Pause & Resume
    Take breaks without losing your progress - pause and resume anytime
  • Finish Your Run
    Press Stop to end your session and save your achievements
  • Export Data
    Download your running data in CSV format for analysis
