Matt Reicher

Biography:

Matt is the creator of Minnesota Then. He has a BA in History from Metropolitan State University and MA in Museum Studies from Oklahoma University. With a passion for history and a nerdy love of museums, Matt understands the importance of making the stories of the past accessible to everyone.
