Matt Reicher

BA History, Metropolitan State University•MA Museum Studies, University of Oklahoma
Matt researches the buildings, blocks, and events that Minnesota's official histories tend to skip. He spends most of his research hours in newspaper archives and county records rather than at a desk, and started Minnesota Then as a way to put those stories back on the map — literally, one location at a time.
