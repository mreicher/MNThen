Matt Reicher

Founder & Historical Researcher

Growing up on the east side of St. Paul, Matt always felt a special connection to our state's rich history. That childhood fascination never faded—instead, it grew into something bigger. After earning a BA in history from Metropolitan State University and MA in Museum Studies from Oklahoma University, Matt knew exactly what he wanted to do: share Minnesota's incredible stories with everyone.

For Matt, history isn't just about memorizing dates or famous names. It's about the real people who lived here, worked here, and built our communities. The small business owner who created a local legacy. The artist remembered fondly years after they've gone. Everyday people who aren't necessarily recognized for the great things they've done beyond the borders of their neighborhood.

When Matt's not digging through old photographs or uncovering forgotten stories in old newspapers, you'll find him canoeing down the St. Croix River or enjoying a craft beer (from Minnesota of course) and talking about local history with anyone willing to listen. Through Minnesota Then, Matt's doing what he loves most—helping fellow Minnesotans connect with our shared past in ways that feel personal, meaningful, and alive.
