Matt Reicher

Founder & Historical Researcher

Passionate about uncovering Minnesota's overlooked narratives that shaped our communities. With a BA in History from Metropolitan State University and MA in Museum Studies from University of Oklahoma, Matt specializes in bringing forgotten stories to light through engaging historical research and storytelling.
